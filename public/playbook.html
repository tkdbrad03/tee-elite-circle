Scramble Rules
1
How a scramble works: All 4 players tee off. The team selects the best shot and everyone plays their next shot from that spot. Repeat until the ball is holed.
2
Tee box: All players use the ladies tees. Each player must contribute a minimum of 2 tee shots per 9 holes.
3
Handicaps: Used for pairing purposes only. Link your GHIN in the app before the event.
4
Mulligans: $5 each. Max 1 per player on the front 9, max 1 per player on the back 9. Team cap of 8 total. Non-transferable between teammates. Not valid on contest holes.
5
Pace of play: We are playing ready golf. If your team falls more than one hole behind the group ahead, a ranger may ask you to skip a hole. Everyone finishes in time for the awards ceremony.
6
Scorecards: One player per team records the score each hole. Digital scorecards will be verified at the clubhouse immediately after your round.
On-Course Contests

Mulligans are not permitted on contest holes. One shot, best effort.
