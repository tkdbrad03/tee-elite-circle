The Experience

Two luxury retreats. One transformative year.

Every element is designed to connect you with the right women, spark the right conversations, and accelerate your next chapter.

I

Two 2-Day Luxury Golf Retreats

Premier courses. Curated pairings. Conversations that matter. These aren't golf trips — they're catalysts for what's next.

II

First Tee

The opening ceremony. The moment you officially enter the room. You'll receive your numbered Tee pin — a symbol that marks you as one of us.

III

Deal on the 9th

A structured networking round where members are intentionally paired to explore business synergies. Deals happen on the course.

IV

The Finished Scorecard

A visualization session rooted in the power of playing from the end. You've already won — now let's see what that looks like.

V

Course Conditions

A life and business diagnostic. What's the current state of your game? Where are the hazards? Where's the opportunity? Let's assess.

VI

Between the Tees

An intimate roundtable where real dilemmas meet real wisdom. Bring your questions. The room will show up for you.

VII

The Back Nine Dinner

The final night. Intimate conversation about wealth, power, regrets, and dreams. This is where the real talk happens.

VIII

The Tee Elite Circle App

Your private community — not a Facebook group. Member directory, wins feed, voice notes, resources, and connection that continues between retreats.
