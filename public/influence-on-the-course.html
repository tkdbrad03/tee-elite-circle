Quick Start Walkthrough (60s)

Master the round by managing the energy, not just the score.

  • Pre-Round: Research your group. Identify one "Resource Gap" you might fill.
  • Holes 1–3: Connection. Low stakes, high rapport. Family, travel, gear.
  • Holes 4–6: Discovery. Deploy the Industry Shift question. Listen 80%.
  • Holes 7–9: Vision. Align your resources with their 3-year goals.
  • 19th Hole: Soft Close. The "Bridge Move"—set the follow-up expectation.

The Wealth Arbitrage Concept

High-performers spend 4 hours in a cart to escape the noise. This is your chance to move from a "vendor" or "peer" to a Strategic Ally. Wealth is built in the spaces between the shots.

The Golden Rule: Ask 2x more questions than you give answers. If you're talking about yourself, you aren't learning their price of entry.
